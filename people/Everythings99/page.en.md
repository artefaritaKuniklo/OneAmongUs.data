## Cat

Enchanted Golden Cat. Well, just hearing a name like that, you can already know, this had to be a cat who loved *Minecraft*.

Perhaps because she had such an adorable name, she often made jokes about it herself.

Once, a mutual on Twitter was hosting a follower milestone giveaway, and among the prizes was a Minecraft apple keychain in transgender flag colours.

She said, “I *am* the Enchanted Golden Apple!” and attached a super fierce kitty sticker.

Another time, someone joked that they were short on money and wanted to “rob a bank[^1] in cyberspace,” calling for people to reply with “1” to join in.

She playfully replied, “1! I’ve got an Enchanted Golden Apple!”

What a mischievous little cat she was.

## Cats

In May 2023, she welcomed a kitten of her own.

> “I have my own kitten now!”

It was a tiny kitten, new to its surroundings and a little scared of her at first.

But before long, the two of them became close friends.

## In Memory

Her passing came suddenly, without any warning at all.

She was very skilled at playing Fall Guys, and had unlocked all of its achievements on Steam at the time.

After her departure, among those who came to mourn her, there were these words from RusndageSNF:

> I suddenly thought of you again… If you were still here, how wonderful that would be. We could queue for Fall Guys together.
> 
> I’d even give you my account... Why... why didn’t I meet you sooner…?
> 
> How did I never get to know you, you little trans girl who played *Fall Guys*…

May there be the games you loved on the other side, the Enchanted Golden Apples you cherished, and the sound of music drifting through the clouds of *Sky*...

[^1]: The “bank robbery” joke is a reference to a scene from *Blue Archive*, see Chapter 1 Episodes 13–15 of the game, or Episode 4 of the anime TV series adaptation.
